ADULT ADHD

Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder

ADHD is defined as high levels of inattentiveness, implusiveness and hyperactivity that begin during childhood persists over time and is present in all aspects of life.
ADHD is basically of 3 types-
a) Predominantly inattentive 
b) Predominantly hyperactive
c) Combined type
Among these the main type of presentation in adults is INATTENTIVENESS

Symptoms of Adult ADHD

INATTENTIVENESS

Difficulty sustaining attention in task
Does not seem to listen when spoken to
Diffculty in organizing a task
Reluctant to engage in task

HYPERACTIVITY

Fidgets with hands and feets
Leaves seat when being seated in 
Runs about in inaprropriate situations 
Talks excessively

IMPLUSIVITY

Blurts out even before question is completed.
Difficulty waiting in a queue.
Interrupts others while speaking
